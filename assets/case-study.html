Avery Dennison

Refining the customer journey

Avery Dennison is a global leader in labelling, packaging materials and solutions. While they enjoy a strong market position and brand awareness, they identified an increase in competition across several key product lines.

W3 were approached to help address this issue as well as identify new growth opportunities through deeper relationships with customers and key supply chain partners.

What we did

W3 worked closely with Avery Dennison’s management team to understand their business objectives, identify key stakeholders and map out the customer journey. We then took them through a service design process to develop a platform that would allow Avery Dennison to speak directly to their end users, shorten sales cycle times and provide value to those in the supply chain they rely on. We also engaged Avery Dennison’s customers and supply chain to identify further opportunities for innovation.

These opportunities were validated with all stakeholders, allowing Avery Dennison to be confident that what we had proposed would make a positive impact with the entire community and generate further increases in sales revenue.
