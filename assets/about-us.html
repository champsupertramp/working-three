Our culture

Our people share a common vision, and have a range of skills that ensure we can think about problems from all angles. We hire people who are smart, determined, and curious.

We maintain an open culture that ensures our people, clients and partners are always encouraged to ask questions and share opinions.

Our values

W3 is governed by our values. They determine how we work, who we hire, the companies we partner with.

UNWAVERING FOCUS ON THE CUSTOMER.

Everything else will follow.

MAKE IT SIMPLE.

Work hard at making things easier.

RESPECT UNIQUENESS.

Provide the environment to empower the individual.

BE CURIOUS.

The market never stops evolving. Stay interested.

DELIVER VALUE.

Focus on the insights, actions and outcomes that improve customers lives.

STAY ENGAGED.

Communicate, collaborate and connect.

BE DETERMINED.

Fight for what's right, despite the challenges.
